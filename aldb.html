About the fixed gun

Cruisers that equip torpedoes have an additional gun that is not visible in the equipment list and cannot be changed. This gun is generally weak and the slot has very low efficiency. However, the gun is fast and has a 360 degrees angle of attack.

This gun gets small improvements from limit breaks and its properties are mostly dependent on the nation of the ship.
